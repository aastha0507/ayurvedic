Mulethi (Licorice)

A natural anti-aging solution, licorice acts as an important ingredient for dull skin.

Manjistha

Called the Indian Madder, Manjistha acts as a skin purifier with astringent properties.

The miraculous blend of Kumkumadi Kesara is a union of sacred herbal essences with Royal Saffron and Rose Water.

The mixture of 21 carefully selected herbal extracts when boiled in water forms a decoction called ‘Kwath’.

This Kwath is then gracefully added to the fusion of Manjistha, Laksha, and Mulethi called ‘Kalka’.

The final blend is then harmoniously bound by Sesame Oil and Goat’s Milk and boiled over flames until only the oil remains.

This golden liquid mixes with a tincture of Royal Saffron and Rose Water, resulting in the creation of Kumkumadi Kesara.

To preserve its efficacy, we place the miraculous elixir in a dark glass bottle.
